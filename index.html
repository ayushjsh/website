Senior Lecturer in Digital Pedagogy

Welcome! I am a Senior Lecturer at the University of Bristol. My work sits at the intersection of Data Science education and student-centered learning environments.

With over a decade of experience in higher education, I am currently the programme director of the highly successful MSc in Data Science programme.
